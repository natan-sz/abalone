🐚 Abalone Age Prediction 🐚

Abalone Fisherman's Best Friend


Abalones - an edible mollusc of warm seas, with a shallow ear-shaped shell lined with mother-of-pearl and pierced with a line of respiratory holes.


This AWS Sagemaker backed tool lets you predict the age of a Abalone shellfish given its sex, dimensions and weight. Use the sliders below to adjust the properties of the desired shellfish to predict the age.
